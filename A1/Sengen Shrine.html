There are more than one thousand "Fuji Sengen Shrines" in Japan, dedicated to the Shinto god of Mount Fuji-Hoki Hananokai Yahime. The Sengen Shrine in Fujiyoshida, formerly known as Kitaguchi Hongu Sengen Shrine, is the main Sengen Shrine on the north side of Mount Fuji. The main palace is the "Fujiyama Hongu Sengen Taisha" in Fujinomiya. Fujiyoshida's Sengen Shrine is located in a dense cedar forest.
